Both companies will welcome back nearly all of the cast members who were performing when Broadway shut down last March, along with exciting fresh faces, including new actors in the starring roles of Simba in The Lion King and Aladdin and Jasmine in Aladdin. The Lion King begins performances on Tuesday, September 14 at the Minskoff Theatre. Alad- din resumes performances on Tuesday, Sep- tember 28 at the New Amsterdam Theatre. Returning to the Broadway cast of the The Lion King are Stephen Carlile as Scar, L. Hamilton as Shenzi and Robb Sapp as Ed. New to the cast is Brandon A. McCall in the role of Simba. McCall makes his Broadway debut in the role, having previously starred in the North American tour.
